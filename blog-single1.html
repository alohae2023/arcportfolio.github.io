Multi-family residential building in wooden technology

By Dimi October 2024 • June 2025
Scroll

"Multi-family residential building project using wood technology, located in Warsaw"

PROJECT OBJECTIVE

The aim of this project is to design and develop a multi-family residential complex using wood technology. The project will be adapted to both the urban and environmental conditions of Warsaw, ensuring comfort, safety, and minimal environmental impact.

PROJECT IDEA

The idea behind this project is to showing that wood technology can also become real and effective solution for the construction of residential buildings mid-floor and even skyscrapers both in large cities and around the world and in Warsaw. For this, the implementation of the project will allow better understand and present knowledge of ecological building design, understand the principles of innovative materials and structures and in practice apply the acquired knowledge and skills.

CHOOSING A PLOT

A plot was selected in the districts of Śródmieście, Wola, and Żoliborz in Warsaw, specifically in the Dworzec Gdański area. This location an ideal undeveloped plot that perfectly suits the goals of this project. The site is situated between Kłopot street and Pamiętajcie o Ogrodach street, within cadastral area 5-01-01. This area encompasses part of Muranów, in the Śródmieście district, and is located in close proximity to the Westfield Arkadia shopping center.

Plot numbers: 13/45 and 13/46 District: Śródmieście, Warsaw Total area: 28,400.0 m²

BUILDING CREATION SHEME
PROJECT OBJECTIVE

As part of my diploma project, I developed a diagram illustrating the building’s development process, showing each stage of its evolution — from the initial concept, through the design phase, to the final form. This diagram not only presents the technical steps of the design process but also tells the story of an idea gradually taking shape. For me, this is a key element of the project, as it allows a holistic view — seeing the building not just as a finished object, but as a living creative process.

The bridge between the two buildings was designed as part of the main concept and an interesting element that complements the whole project. He has not only connect blocks, but also be a place of rest and meetings for residents. In the center provides greenery and panoramic views of the area. Additional advantage there is a sliding glass panel system that allows you to open or close space depending on the weather – on warm or cool days.
