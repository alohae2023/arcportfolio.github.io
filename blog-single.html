Modern Office Building

By Dimi on March, 2024 • 5 mins read
Scroll

"Horizon Heights" 2024

This building stands out with its unique design, featuring rotating anti-solar panels that automatically adjust to the position of the Sun.

It is located in the Prague Port area in the heart of Warsaw, surrounded by water on three sides.

Beneath the site, there is an underground parking facility that accommodates 72 cars, 20 bicycles, and 3 motorcycles.

The ground floor houses the main entrance, leading to a reception area and a coworking space.

Modern architecture also attempted to help solve a practical problem: a boom in the urban population. Immigration and new industries like steelmaking were attracting people from rural areas to cities, and there was a need for new offices, factories, and housing to keep up with growth.
