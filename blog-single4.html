"Glamping park" 2023

Glamping is a form of camping that offers more luxurious facilities than traditional camping.

It provides a luxury experience where campers stay in semi-indoor accommodations rather than the usual tents or sleeping bags.

Unlike traditional camping, glamping typically includes amenities such as beds, electricity, and indoor plumbing.

While not a new concept, contemporary glamping sets itself apart from the luxurious camps of the past by focusing on sustainability, aesthetics, and an enhanced user experience.

These structures are thoughtfully designed to combine comfort with a connection to nature.
