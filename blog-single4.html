Square in Egypt

By Dimi • November 2023
Scroll

"Square in Egypt"

Space is one of the most fundamental concepts in architecture, often described by architectural theorists as the organization of spaces that hold meaning for people.

Architecture is about more than just the physical structure—it’s about crafting expressive spaces that are not only experienced visually but also evoke emotional connections.

Designing multi-functional urban squares requires the careful integration of architectural and urban design principles.

Architects must thoughtfully consider scale, proportion, and spatial organization to ensure that the square complements its surroundings. Striking a balance between form and function is essential for these spaces to successfully serve their varied purposes.

Public space embodies a shared value, serving as a gathering place, even more so in the information age. The vision of a common area reflects and facilitates the well-being of society. Designing urban spaces involves adhering to fundamental principles that ensure they function effectively and inclusively.
